Services Offered

Beauty parlors offer a range of services and treatments designed to enhance and maintain one's physical appearance. These establishments often provide services such as haircuts, hairstyling, hair coloring, manicures, pedicures, facials, makeup application, waxing, and more. To attract customers and promote their services, beauty parlors often offer various types of offers.

Lash Extentions

Lash extensions are a popular cosmetic procedure where individual artificial lashes are meticulously applied to natural lashes, creating a fuller and longer appearance. These extensions are typically made of synthetic fibers or real human hair and are carefully bonded to the natural lashes using a safe adhesive.

Nail Extentions

Nail extensions, also known as artificial nails or nail enhancements, are a popular beauty treatment that involves adding length, strength, and shape to natural nails. This procedure is typically done using various techniques such as acrylic, gel, or fiberglass.



Makeup is a transformative artistry that allows individuals to enhance their features, express their personal style, and boost their confidence. It encompasses a wide range of products and techniques that can be applied to the face, eyes, lips, and more. Foundation helps create a smooth and even complexion, while concealer camouflages imperfections.
